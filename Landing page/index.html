    CRICKET

Cricket, a beloved sport worldwide, is a captivating blend of strategy, skill, and teamwork. Played
between two teams, it involves batting, bowling, and fielding. The objective is to score more runs
than the opponent within a designated time. Matches can span hours or days, offering intense
moments and thrilling displays of athleticism. With its rich history and fervent fan base, cricket
remains a cultural cornerstone in many nations.
